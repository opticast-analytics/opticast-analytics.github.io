What we do

We unlock your energy system's potential by optimizing its operation following these three steps:

  1. We curate all data pipelines necessary to model energy markets.
  2. We forecast electricity prices of both spot and balancing markets.
  3. We optimize your energy system's operation to minimize costs and maximize profits.
  4. We provide you the optimal operation plan in real time for you to execute.
  5. We provide you with a real time dashboard, that benchmarks our approach against different baselines.
